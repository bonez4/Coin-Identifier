Obverse Legend

HADRIANVS Hadrianus — the emperor’s personal name
AVGVSTVS Augustus — “The Revered One” — the supreme imperial title

Obverse Image

Laureate bust of Hadrian facing right, with drapery on left shoulder. The laurel wreath honors military achievement and divine favor.

Reverse Legend

LIBERTAS Libertas — the Roman goddess of freedom and personal liberty
RESTITVTA Restituta — “Restored” — implying Hadrian restored liberty to the empire

Reverse Image

Libertas standing left, holding a pileus (the felt cap given to freed slaves, symbolizing liberty) and a long rod or scepter. This type celebrated Hadrian’s reputation for restoring civil liberties and easing the severity of Trajan’s later policies.
