Obverse Legend

IMP Imperator — Supreme military commander
CAESAR Caesar — Imperial dynastic title
VESPASIANVS Vespasianus — the emperor’s personal name
AVG Augustus — “The Revered One”

Obverse Image

Laureate head of Vespasian facing right. The laurel wreath signifies military triumph and imperial authority. Vespasian rose from humble origins to found the Flavian dynasty after the chaos of the Year of Four Emperors.

Reverse Legend

IVDAEA Judaea — the Roman province conquered after the Jewish revolt of AD 66–70

Reverse Image

The iconic "Judaea Capta" type: a mourning female figure (personification of Judaea) seated right in an attitude of grief beneath a trophy of captured arms. This type celebrated Rome’s suppression of the Jewish revolt and the destruction of the Temple in AD 70.
